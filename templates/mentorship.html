2017-18 BEA MENTORSHIP PROGRAM

DEADLINE TO APPLY: FRIDAY, OCTOBER 13TH, 2017

MENTORSHIP MEET & GREET: TUESDAY, OCTOBER 17TH, 2017

Looking for a mentor who will help guide you academically and professionally?

We have the perfect program for you! BEA is hosting its inaugural Mentorship Program. Mentees who are ideally Freshmen and Sophomores will be paired up with a mentor who is either a BEA Officer or a BEA Alum based on career path and mutual interests. Mentees and mentors will meet throughout the school year depending on availability, and BEA will be hosting a few events for mentees and mentors to connect with one another.

Benefits to this program include having the opportunity to interact with upperclassmen and recent graduates who are making/made it through college, which is a great opportunity to ask mentors not just career questions, but academic questions as well. Speaking of which, the program emphasizes a safe space for mentees to ask mentors questions.
