Membership is free.

BEA doesn't believe in having to pay for your dose of ethics. Below are three ways you can get involved with BEA without being an officer!

Meetings.

There are three different kinds of meetings we host on Tuesdays at 6pm - ethical discussion, speaker, and social meetings. Feel free to join in at any of our meetings!

Programs.

BEA hosts two significant programs during the year - Mentorship Program and Ethics Case Competition. Become a mentee and/or compete or volunteer for the case competition!

Volunteering.

Not only do we believe in ethical business practices, we also believe in giving back. BEA hosts one volunteer event meeting each quarter. Join us for a good cause and great company!
