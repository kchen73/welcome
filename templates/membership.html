Membership is free.

BEA doesn't believe in having to pay for your dose of ethics. Below are three ways you can get involved with BEA without being an officer!

Meetings.

There are three different kinds of meetings we host on Tuesdays at 6pm - ethical discussion, speaker, and social meetings. Feel free to join in at any of our meetings!

Committees.

BEA hosts two large events during spring quarter - Ethics Case Competition and Amazing Race. Planning for these two events begin early during fall quarter, and anyone interested can join one or both these committees!

Volunteering.

Not only do we believe in ethical business practices, we also believe in giving back. Starting this year, BEA will host one volunteer event meeting each quarter. Join us for a good cause and great company!
